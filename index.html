Productivity S.O.S. Data

Welcome to the Productivity Smart Office Sensor Data website. Here you can access the data available on your Netatmo weather station system. All you need to do is enter your access token below to list the different devices registered to you. Then by entering a date range no greater then four months, you can pull all the data locally to be displayed on the charts page. Make sure your access token is up to date! Click here to view the steps on how to refresh a token. This website will retrieve all the devices listed under favourites for the users token.

The maximun number of months allowed is four
